    张嘉宸 (Jiachen Zhang)

    张嘉宸，毕业于上海对外经贸大学，金融学专业，获上海市优秀毕业生，研究生国家奖学金等荣誉。 主要研究领域为实证资产定价和金融市场微观结构，相关成果发表在Economics Letters,   Applied Economics,  The North American Journal of Economics and Finance 和 《经济管理》等期刊，此外还有多篇工作论文在 Journal of International Money and Finance 和《会计研究》等期刊审稿中。

    Jiachen Zhang, aka Lester Zhang, is previously a master's degree student in finance at Shanghai University of International Business and Economics. With a focus on empirical asset pricing and market microstructure, his work has been published at journals including Economics Letters, Applied Economics, The North American Journal of Economics and Finance, 《经济管理》, and/or under review at Journal of International Money and Finance, 《会计研究》.
